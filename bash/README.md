### 发布脚本包含三个模块：
* api[提供对其它部门的统一接口]
* service[dubbo 服务组件]
* webapp[提供前台服务对接接口]

[部署api]
bash/deploy-api.sh

[部署service]
bash/deploy-service.sh

[部署web]
bash/deploy-web.sh


--------------------------------
[启动api-webapp]
bash/startup-api.sh

[停止api-webapp]
bash/shutdown-api.sh

--------------------------------

[启动service]
bash/startup-service.sh

[停止service]
bash/shutdown-service.sh

--------------------------------

[启动web]
bash/startup-web.sh

[停止web]
bash/shutdown-web.sh

------------------------------
